3Vision Behind IvariesvariestouchI

Caster gets a vision of up to 1 minute per level into the past. The caster must select the point in the past to be viewed (and the vision will be accurate within one minute). The vision must be associated with a person, place, or object touched at the time of casting. The vision includes sight and sound. The vision will last up to 1 round per level if the caster concentrates and remains inactive. Time passes at a rate of one round of real time for 10 rounds of vision.

5Vision Guide1 spellvariesselfI

This spell gives the caster the ability to scan the past by "feeling the temporal location for significant events". This translates to the caster knowing the approximate time-frames of significant events, without knowing the nature of the events. If the caster casts a Vision Behind spells within one minute of casting this spell, she will be able to see the specific events.
