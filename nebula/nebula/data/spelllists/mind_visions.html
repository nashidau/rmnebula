1. Origins — Gives a general idea of the place of origin of an item.

2. Detect Curse — Detects if a curse is upon a person, place or object.

3. Vision Behind I — Caster gets a vision of up to 1 minute per level into the past. The caster must select the point in the past to be viewed (and the vision will be accurate within one minute). The vision must be associated with a person, place, or object touched at the time of casting. The vision includes sight and sound. The vision will last up to 1 round per level if the caster concentrates and remains inactive. Time passes at a rate of one round of real time for 10 rounds of vision.

4. Power Lore — Gives the origin of an item's power.

5. Vision Guide — This spell gives the caster the ability to scan the past by "feeling the temporal location for significant events". This translates to the caster knowing the approximate time-frames of significant events, without knowing the nature of the events. If the caster casts a Vision Behind spells within one minute of casting this spell, she will be able to see the specific events.

6. Item Vision — Gives a vision of a significant event in the item's past.

7. Vision Behind II — As Vision Behind I, except time range is up to 10 minutes per level (with an accuracy of +5 minutes) and the trance can last up to 1 minute per level.

8. Vision Enhancement — When cast before a Vision Behind spell, this allows the caster to perceive the past using one (and only one) additional sense, or to use one and only one perceptive skill (in addition to the normal vision and hearing).

9. Vision Behind III — As Vision Behind I, except time range is up to 1 hour per level (with an accuracy of +30 minutes) and the trance can last up to 5 minute per level.

10. Delving — Gives significant details about an item's construction and purpose (not specific powers).
