Other Programs

The Nebula Suite is composed of a number of different programs. They are:

  • Nebula: Character editor - the main program.
  • Stardust: Spell list editor.
  • Alchemist: Item editor and treasure generator.
  • World Builder: Genre (world) file editor.
  • Genome: A race editor.
  • Guild: A professions editor.
  • Coach: A Training package editor.

Nebula

Nebula is the center-piece of the aplication suite. Nebula is designed to allow you to create, manage and manipulate rolemaster characters.

Status: Mostly complete.

Stardust

Stardust is a spell list editor. The name comes from what nebula's are made from (star dust) and as well as vague notions on the idea of fairy dust (the stuff of magic in Peter Pan).

Stardust allows you to read, modify and create spell lists. More information on the spell list can be found at spelllist XML page.

Status: Mostly complete: implemented

  • Loading, saving etc
  • Edit spell paramaters (name, range, description)
  • Edit spell lists paramaters (name etc)
  • Delete spells

Unimplemented:

  • Spell list notes
  • Spell references (eg As Foo I, but 100' level)

Alchemist

Alchemist is priamrily written by Sylvain Glaize. It allows you create, modify, and view all the properties of items - from description, inteligence, magical and non-magical bonuses, plus pretty much anything you can think of.

Status: Partially complete.

World Builder

Status: Partially complete. Implemented:
